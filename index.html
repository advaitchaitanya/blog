इति शिवम्

May there be goodness for all.

Advait Chaitanya is a resident acharya of Advaita Vedanta at the Sambodh Center for Human Excellence in Kalamazoo, MI under the guidance of Swami Bodhananda Saraswati. He offers regular classes both in-person and online on the Upanishad and Yoga disciplines. He also has a passion for expressing the teachings through writing and producing music.
